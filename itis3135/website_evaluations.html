Website Evaluations

Apple

The Apple website is a prime example of a clean, user-centric, and visually appealing website that adheres to modern web design standards.

  • Contrast: The Apple website uses high contrast between text and background, making it easy to read. The use of black, white, and gray ensures a professional look.
  • Repetition: The design maintains consistent elements such as fonts, colors, and button styles across pages, creating a unified experience.
  • Alignment: Every element is perfectly aligned, giving the website a structured and clean appearance. Navigation menus, images, and text are neatly placed.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped together effectively. For example, product details and pricing are placed near the product images, enhancing usability.

Craigslist

Craigslist, while functional, is a notorious example of a website that neglects modern design principles in favor of minimalism.

  • Contrast: The lack of color and low contrast between text and background can make the content difficult to read and visually unappealing.
  • Repetition: The site does not have a consistent style for headings, links, or other elements, making it look outdated and disorganized.
  • Alignment: Many elements feel misaligned or haphazardly placed, resulting in a chaotic appearance that can confuse users.
  • Proximity: Content is crammed together with little spacing, making it hard to distinguish between sections, especially on the homepage.
