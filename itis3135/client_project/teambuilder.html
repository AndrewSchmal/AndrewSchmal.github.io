The best team in the game at launch, and while powercreep has made it less dominant, it's still the best ice team by far. Ellen does insane damage, Lycaon is there to build stun and increase the enemy's ice weakness, and Soukaku is there to just stay on the field with "Fly The Flag" and buff your on-field agent. Sharkboo is the best offensive bangboo, but you can use Penguinboo if you don't have it.
