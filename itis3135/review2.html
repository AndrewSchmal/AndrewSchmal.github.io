Additional Feedback

  • Strength: The website feels polished, with good organization and styling. The color scheme works well to highlight content. All of the links in the header and footer seem to work, and the styling is consistent across different pages.
  • Improvement: While the color scheme works well overall, the graph being bright white compared to the more natural colors of the rest of the website does stand out a bit. Maybe consider changing the color of the graphs away from white. Additionally, adding a little more spacing between the footer links would make them easier to distinguish.
  • Suggestion: If you wanna get fancy, you could try adding some responsive hover-over type styling or transitions to the navigation or tables for a modern feel. Also ensure responsiveness by testing on various screen sizes and making adjustments if necessary, particularly for the table content or graphs, which might overflow on smaller devices.
